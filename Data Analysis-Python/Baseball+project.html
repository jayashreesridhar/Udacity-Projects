Factors Influencing Salary among players¶

I would like to analyse the factors that would contribute to the salary among players.On looking at the dataset I would like to analyse and answer the following questions.¶

1.Does maximum appearance in games guarantee high salary?¶

2.Does runs scored have an effect on salary?¶

3.Impact of fielding position on salary¶

4.Impact of batting hand on salary¶

5.How salary is dictributed across age of players¶

To answer these questions I am considering the following csv files from baseballdatabank-master_2016-03-02 dataset¶

1.Salaries.csv¶

2.Appearances.csv¶

3.Batting.csv¶

4.Fielding.csv¶

5.Master.csv¶

#Function to find correlation between  2 variables
def correlation(x, y):
    std_x=(x-x.mean())/x.std(ddof=0)
    std_y=(y-y.mean())/y.std(ddof=0)
    return (std_x *std_y).mean()

I have analyzed each questions and some of my hypothesis are correct while few of them are wrong.¶

Questions where Hypothesis Retained¶

Total runs scored by a player does have a positve impact on his salary.However the impact is not stronger.¶

Field position does have an impact on salary.We have seen average salary across different field positions.The difference in salary between highest and lowest average salary position is notable.¶

Age of a player has an effect on salary.From the plot we could see that till some points it has positive linear relationship beyond which it goes to negative linear relationship.But the overall effect is positive as age of most players fall under posive relationship bucket.¶

Questions where Hypothesis failed to prove¶

Total games played,games where player batted and where he appeared on defense doesn't have strong postive linear relationship with salary.¶

Players batting hand doesn't have an effect on salary.Average salary for left,right and both hands are almost same¶
